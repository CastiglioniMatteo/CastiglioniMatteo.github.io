About Me

I'm a PhD student at the Department of Electronics, Information and Bioengineering (DEIB) at Politecnico di Milano, where I'm advised by Prof. Nicola Gatti. My current research focuses on Artificial Intelligence, especially Algorithmic Game Theory, Social Influence and Computational Complexity. I am intrested in the computational complexity of finding equilibria, and the development of efficient algorithm to compute equilibria. In particular, my research focuses on Leader-follower games, signaling games and election manipulation through social influence.
